Our Story

VAUX began as a small collective of designers and curators who believed that everyday objects should be made with care. We focus on materials, finish, and fit — not flash. Our range is intentionally curated so each piece earns its place in your home.

What We Value

  • Longevity — products built to be used and loved.
  • Transparency — clear sourcing and fair relationships.
  • Design — considered details that stand the test of time.
